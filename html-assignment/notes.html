5 HTML tags

Image
Image is a singular tag (self-closing) and needs an attribute to tell it what image to show on your web page: src (source) to provide the relative or absolute path to the image and alt (alternate text) to provide a description of the image for search engines, screen readers, and in case the image does not load.
