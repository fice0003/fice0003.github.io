About Me

Namaste. My name is Jennifer, but please call me Jen. I was born and raised in Ottawa, so I’m a champ at handling winter weather. In fact, when I was in 10th grade, my first driving test was done during a snowstorm (no biggie). I’m not really into winter sports, except for cross-country skiing.
I have spent the last decade without any clear direction on a career path and have worked in many different roles and industries. I am interested in a career where I can be creative, work remotely, and not be stuck in a mundane routine. This is why I have chosen the Interactive Media Design Program.
